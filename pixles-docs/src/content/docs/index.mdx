## Why Pixles?

Pixles is designed for photographers, videographers, and visual artists who need more than just cloud storage. With powerful organization tools, blazing-fast performance, and complete privacy control, Pixles is the photo management solution you've been searching for.

### Key benefits:

- **No data limits**: Store unlimited photos and videos
- **Full privacy**: Your data stays on your servers
- **AI-powered**: Intelligent tagging and face recognition
- **RAW support**: Full management of professional file formats
- **Modern UI**: Beautiful, responsive interface
- **Open source**: Community-driven development
